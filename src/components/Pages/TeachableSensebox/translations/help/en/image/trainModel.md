During training, the model learns to distinguish the defined classes based on the captured images.

# What happens during training?

The model goes through several **epochs** – in each epoch, it is presented with all the training images and adjusts its internal parameters slightly. This is repeated until the model no longer improves or we reach the maximum number of epochs (in this case, 70).

# Notes

- Training runs entirely in the browser – your images are never uploaded.
- After training you can immediately test the model using the live preview.
