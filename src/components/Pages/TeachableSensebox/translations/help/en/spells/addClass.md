You can define between **two** and **five** spells yourself. To do this, add a new spell and record some **example movements** for that spell. Try to record at least 5 example movements per spell.

A spell movement can, for example, be a **shape** (“circle”, “square”, etc.), a **direction** (“left to right”, “top to bottom”, etc.) or **numbers** and **letters**.

![senseBox wand](/media/teachable/spell.gif)

**Tip:** Try to create simple and distinct spells. The more complex or similar the spells the harder it will be for the model to correctly classify them.
