A class represents a category that your model should recognize. For example, animals, colours, objects, people, gestures. You need at least **2 classes** to train a model.

# Tips

- Ensure that the classes are as **distinct** as possible so that the model can clearly distinguish between them. It is important that the classes differ visually.
- Collect at least **100 images per class** for good results. The more images, the better.
- Ensure **variety** in the images: different angles, lighting conditions and distances improve the model’s accuracy.
- The camera only sees in **black and white** and in a **low resolution**. Make sure that your chosen classes also look different in this resolution and in black and white.
- Try to imagine that you were learning a class based on the images captured. Can you, as a human, distinguish the images clearly? If not, you should review your images again.
